Information We Collect

Personal Information

We may collect personal details, such as:

 

  • Name, date of birth, gender
  • Email address, residential address, and contact numbers
  • PAN number, Aadhaar details, and financial data
  • Employment details, salary slips, and bank statements
  • Information you voluntarily provide through forms or during communication with our team

 

Non-Personal Information

We automatically collect data when you visit our site, such as:

 

  • Browser type and version

  • Usage patterns, pages visited, session durations, and frequency of visits
  • Cookies and similar technologies to improve user experience

 

How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to:

 

  • Provide and improve our financial products and services
  • Assess your eligibility and process your loan applications or credit card requests
  • Verify your identity to prevent fraud and ensure security
  • Communicate important updates, offers, or changes in policies
  • Respond to your queries, requests, or complaints effectively
  • Comply with regulatory and legal obligations

 

Non-personal information helps us enhance our website, understand user behavior, and optimize our offerings.

Sharing of Information

We may share your personal information in the following cases:

 

  • With Financial Partners and Institutions: For loan approvals, credit risk assessment, processing, and fulfilling services.
  • Service Providers: We may share data with third-party providers for website hosting, data analytics, security, and customer service support.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information to comply with applicable laws, regulations, court orders, or lawful requests from authorities.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or restructuring, your data may be transferred to a successor entity.

 

We ensure that all third parties receiving your data are compliant with data protection and confidentiality standards.

We implement industry-standard security measures, including encryption, firewalls, and secure servers, to safeguard your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or misuse.

Retention of Data

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary for providing services, resolving disputes, compliance with legal obligations, and business purposes. Once the retention period expires, your data is securely disposed of in accordance with applicable regulations.

Your Rights

You have the right to:
